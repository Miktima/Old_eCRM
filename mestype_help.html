TYPES / CONTENTS OF MESSAGES AND STRUCTURE OF ANSWERS

Type / abbr.Essence of the message Typical contentStructure of the answer
PROBLEM (PR) Notification of abnormal program (sample, website, etc) behaviour, unambiguously interpreted as a bug. Description of conditions and symptoms of the problem. Features that are unavailable due to the problem. Detailed description of conditions and consequences of the error. Advice on how to workaround the problem. Our bug: status/resolution, availability of patches (new versions) or thanks for problem report. Other's bug: clarification and redirection to relevant bodies.
UNEXPECTED BEHAVIOUR (UB) Notification of program (sample, website, etc) behaviour, not matching user's logic or habits. Description of conditions and symptoms of the situation. Features that work not as the user expects. Detailed description of the situation, explanation of its logic, advice on the recommended order of actions.
HOW TO (HT) Request for information needed to achieve certain result. Description of initial and desired state, information of user's capabilities. Step-by-step procedure of achieving the final state
QUERY (QR) Request for advice what to do, connected with incomplete understanding of situation. Incomplete HOW TO (the problem is not fully described) or obscure, not concisely formulated question. An attempt to expound the essence of the question, as far as it can be understood from the message. If the situation is connected with typical problem/behaviour - detailed solution. If not - listing of 3-4 possible options without details.
SUGGESIONS (SG) Declaration of a new idea. Proposals for improvements of existing program (sample, website), or co-operation. Acknowledgements, confirmation of transferring of the proposals to corresponding services.
INFORMATION (IN) Transfer of information, which not required immediate action. Technical information, expression of sb point of view / emotions (compliments, insults). May remain not replied or thanks for valuable information or general advice.
Sales opportunity Request for information on products pricing, purchase opportunities and the ways of payments and delivery. Incomplete SALES Request (the client asks how to purchase PG's products, price, delivery methods, etc) Detailed descriprion on ordering procedure: link to PG's pricing and ordering information, edu discounts if needed, sending Order Form, delivery prices.
Sales reports Automated sales reports from on-line shops: Buyonet, DR, Regnow, Handagoo, PocketGear Date of purchase, product, version, name, e-mail and address of the client, quantity of purchased products, version (downloadable/reg.key), ID, Reg.Key. Standard Welcome letter has to be sent.
